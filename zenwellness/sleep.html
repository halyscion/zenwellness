Sleep Improvement Strategies

Consistent Sleep Schedule

Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends. This regulates your body's internal clock and can help you fall asleep and stay asleep for the night.

Create a Restful Environment

Keep your bedroom cool, dark and quiet. Consider using room-darkening shades, earplugs, a fan or other devices to create an environment that suits your needs.

Limit Stimulants

Avoid caffeine after 2pm and limit alcohol, which can disrupt sleep later in the night. Nicotine is also a stimulant that can interfere with sleep.

Digital Detox Before Bed

Turn off electronic devices at least 30 minutes before bed as the blue light can disrupt melatonin production. Try reading a book instead.
