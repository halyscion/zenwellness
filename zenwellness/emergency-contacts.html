🆘 Emergency Contacts

If you or someone you know is in crisis or needs urgent mental health support, please use one of the services listed below. These emergency contacts are available to provide confidential help, guidance, and professional assistance.

🔥 Immediate Emergency Services

  • Emergency Services (999): For life-threatening emergencies.
    Call: 999
  • NHS 111 – Non-Emergency Help: For urgent mental health concerns.
    Call: 111 | Visit NHS 111

💬 Mental Health Support Helplines

  • Samaritans: 24/7 support for emotional distress.
    Call: 116 123 | Visit Samaritans
  • Mind InfoLine: Mental health info and guidance.
    Call: 0300 123 3393 (Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm) | Visit Mind
  • Shout – Crisis Text Line: Free 24/7 text support.
    Text: SHOUT to 85258
  • Nightline (Student Service): Confidential listening run by students.
    Check your university website for availability.

🧭 Cardiff-Based Services

  • Cardiff Met University Wellbeing Service: Mental health support for Cardiff Met students.
    Visit Wellbeing Service
  • University GP Services: Book appointments or get referrals via your registered GP.
    Use NHS 111 to find a GP
  • South Wales Mental Health Crisis Team: Urgent assessments outside working hours.
    Access through NHS 111 or your GP.

🚨 Domestic Abuse & Safety Support

  • National Domestic Abuse Helpline: Free, 24/7 support.
    Call: 0808 2000 247 | Visit Website
  • Respect Men’s Advice Line: For men experiencing domestic abuse.
    Call: 0808 8010327 | Visit Website

Disclaimer: ZenWellness is not a diagnostic or crisis response tool. If you are in immediate danger, please contact emergency services or go to the nearest A&E.

About ZenWellness

ZenWellness is a digital mental health self-help platform created specifically for university students in South Wales. Our mission is to support students’ emotional well-being by providing a confidential, secure space for journaling, accessing reliable information, and finding local resources.

🌿 What We Offer

  • 📓 Journaling Tool: Privately track your mood, thoughts, and progress over time.
  • 🤖 Chatbot Guidance: Talk to our AI-based bot for basic advice, grounding techniques, or emotional check-ins.
  • 📚 Mental Health Info: Learn about symptoms, thought patterns, stress signs, and more.
  • 📍 Local Services: Find Cardiff-based GPs, therapy clinics, and university wellbeing support.
  • 🔒 Privacy First: All data is secure and confidential in compliance with GDPR policies.

🚫 What We Are Not

ZenWellness is not a diagnostic tool or a substitute for clinical therapy or emergency care. If you are in crisis, please use our Emergency Contacts page or call 999 for immediate support.

🎯 Our Purpose

University life can be overwhelming, and we understand the need for emotional support that’s flexible, personal, and stigma-free. ZenWellness empowers students to take control of their mental health journey, with a space designed for reflection, support, and growth.
